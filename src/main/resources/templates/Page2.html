Step 2 Assembling Your Aquarium

Apply the silicone

Start with the bottom piece, applying a thin and continuous strip of silicone along the top, about 2mm away from the edge (where the front pane of glass will rest on it). The strip of silicone should be about 3mm in diameter.
Put the front pane in place

With the strip of silicone along the front edge of the base, place the front piece of glass into place, pressing it down firmly but gently. Hold it there briefly, adhere the rest of the tape up the sides, and it should stay up.

Begin assembling the sides

With your caulk gun in hand, run another thin line of silicone (again, 2mm from the edge), along the sides. Then repeat along the inside edge of the front pane
Finish with the back pane of glass.

Now that you're getting the hang of the caulk gun, run your last 3mm-wide lines of silicone along the edge of the bottom pane (2mm from the edge) and along the inside edges of the back panel.
Allow the silicone to dry and set.

Most types of silicone dry within 24-48 hours.[5] It will harden even more as time goes on, so if you can resist, don't fill it with water for a good week or so.
